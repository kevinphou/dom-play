"Memory of the Sailboat"

Heinz: Welcome aboard, Miss Geiringer. I am your captain. We will be sailing at 0900 hours. The winds are moderate from the southwest.

Eva: Aye, aye, Captain!

Heinz: And welcome Sanne and Herman. Are you ready to set sail?

Sanne: I am so excited. I brought a small picnic to share. With cake

Herman: So did I. We will have plenty of food.

Heinz: Ha! I’ve seen how much you can eat

Herman: I’m growing!

Heinz: We all are.
